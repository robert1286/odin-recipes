Banana bread

Description

"This is the only banana bread recipe I will make," says Anna Mae Dinio-Bloch. "It's the perfect density and 'sweetness' with the chocolate chips added. A definite MUST have in your recipe box!"

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
    • /
  • 1/2 cup butter

Directions

step 1

preheat to 325 degrees F

step 2

Mix flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a bowl. Stir bananas, milk, and cinnamon in another bowl. Beat sugar and butter together in a third bowl with an electric mixer until light and fluffy; add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir banana mixture into butter mixture; mix in flour mixture until just blended. Fold in chocolate chips; pour batter into the prepared loaf pan

step 3

bake for about 70 minutes, cool for 10 minutes
