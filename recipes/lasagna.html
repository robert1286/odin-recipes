lasagna

Description

Super simple recipe for lasagna.

Ingrediants

  • beef
  • marinara
  • cheese
  • eggs
  • seasonings
  • lasagna noodles

directions

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer.
  2. Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the Parmesan, and seasonings.
  3. assemble lasagna
  4. bake covered for 40 min then 10 uncovered
